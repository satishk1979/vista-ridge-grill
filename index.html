The hidden gem

Best breakfast in Cedar Park is inside a gas station.

Tucked inside Fizola Market at the Valero, Vista Ridge Grill is the kind of spot regulars swear by. Family-owned and women-owned, with owner TJ bringing real Chicago roots — that's why the Italian beef is the real deal.

Everything's made fresh and made big: individually fried eggs on the breakfast tacos, seasoned potatoes crisped to perfection, brisket and bacon cooked just right, and homemade hot sauces folks drive across town for.

Grab it to go, pull up for curbside, or eat in. First time? As the crew says — "You'll be back."
